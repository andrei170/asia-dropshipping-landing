FAQ

Common Questions

Is this just for Black women?

The community center is Black women in ecom. All women are welcome. The perspective, community norms, and brand aesthetics center Black female founders — that's the whole differentiator from other ecom programs.

How much capital do I need?

Engine 3 (digital products) starts under $50. Engine 2 (POD) starts under $100. Engine 1 (branded dropshipping) $500-$2,000. Pick your engine based on capital and time availability.

How long until I profit?

Engine 3 can profit week 1-2. Engine 2 usually profits month 2-3. Engine 1 profits month 3-6. All three require consistency. None are overnight.

Is ecom still viable in 2026?

More than it was in 2020. Margins are higher because tools are better. Audiences are more niche-receptive. The landscape favors specific brands over generic dropshippers.

Who is this NOT for?

Anyone who wants fake flex. Anyone looking for get-rich-quick. Anyone who doesn't want to post content. Ecom is work. The soft life is the reward.
